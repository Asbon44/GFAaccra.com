Principal's Welcome

Welcome to General Fashion Academy! We are thrilled to have you here. Our institution is dedicated to nurturing raw talent into world-class fashion expertise. Under our guidance, we emphasize creativity, professionalism, and entrepreneurship. Step into a world of vibrant fabrics and limitless potential.

Our History

General Fashion Academy started its journey in 1994. Through decades of dedication to the craft, we have shaped numerous professionals passing through our doors. As early adopters of the modern fashion curriculum in the Kumasi Metropolis, we've remained a beacon of design excellence and technical finesse.
