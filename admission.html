Admission Process

We appreciate your interest in our institution. We are excited to introduce you to the general fashion academy community, and our website is a terrific location to learn more about everything we have to offer.

How to get the forms:

  • Purchase application form at the front desk of the school.
  • Pay through Via Momo 055 668 9796 (General Fashion Academy) Send a screenshot to 024 323 2532 of Momo transaction.

Cost & Payments

ITEMS COST (GH)
Application Form 100.00
Tuition Fees 2,500.00 / YEAR
SRC Dues 200.00 per Semester
Miscellaneous(Uniform and Student_ID) 600.00
Maintenance Fee 500.00
Hostel Fee 1,500.00

Important Notice

  • Application forms should be purchased only at school premises.
  • Filled forms should be submitted in person at school premises.
  • Tuition fee shall be paid every year.
  • Fees paid are not refundable.
  • Tuition, miscellaneous & maintenance fees must be paid at the office or through momo or at the bank.
  • Miscellaneous covers school uniform and student ID card.
  • You can pay 70% of the tution (or full amount)
