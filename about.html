About Us

General Fashion Academy is a small-scale dressmaking firm that has been in business since 1994. Quality, innovation, and passion define everything we do.

It is one of the 15 companies selected in 2002 by the government of Ghana to launch the President’s Special Initiative (PSI) on textile and garment to take advantage of AGOA (Africa Growth & Opportunity Act) & also one of the most recognized dressmaking firms in the Kumasi Metropolis.
